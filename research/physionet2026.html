    George B. Moody PhysioNet Challenge 2026

    Decoding Cognitive Impairment from Overnight Polysomnography

    A research note (in progress) on decoding cognitive impairment from overnight polysomnography (PSG). Will cover nonlinear EEG dynamics (recurrence quantification, multiscale and permutation entropy) stratified across N2, N3, and REM sleep; brain–heart coupling between HRV and EEG delta-band activity; slow-wave dissipation and REM-sleep muscle atonia as biomarkers of neurodegenerative decline; and integration of pretrained EEG foundation-model embeddings (BIOT, REVE) within a cross-site leak-free ensemble. Collaboration with the Clifford Lab (Emory / Georgia Tech NeuroSignals).
