Motor Imagery EEG Decoding — ISLA 2026

Collaboration with Pedro L. C. Rodrigues (Inria, statify team)

Apr – May 2026

Developed Cross-Agent Multi-Family Evidence Fusion, a framework for trial-level calibration in small-sample EEG decoding of motor imagery. Combines a deep convolutional network (EEGNet) with four classical spectral–spatial estimators (CSP, CSSP, inter-channel phase-locking, and a hybrid) and treats cross-method disagreement as a calibration signal. Introduces a retrieval-based test-time augmentation that diagnoses predictions unsupported by neighbours in the model's own feature space — surfacing non-sensorimotor features that the deep network latches onto but that the spectral families correctly reject. Candidate submissions are ranked by a Poisson–Binomial tail probability, favouring parsimonious corrections.
